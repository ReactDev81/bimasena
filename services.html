Our Services
Empowering Your Financial Success

At Bimasena Artha Internasional, we offer a comprehensive range of interbank money market services tailored to meet the needs of financial institutions across Indonesia. With decades of experience and a commitment to excellence, our services are designed to ensure seamless, secure, and effective transactions.

Foreign Exchange

Bimasena offers a range of foreign exchange services, including spot, tom, today, swap, forward, and DNDF. These services provide clients with flexible and efficient solutions for their currency trading and hedging needs.

Bond and Securities

At Bimasena, we provide a comprehensive suite of services in bonds and securities trading, catering to a variety of investment needs.

Other Services

Our additional services are designed to support and enhance the core operations of our clients, ensuring they have access to critical information and infrastructure

Foreign Exchange

Spot: Immediate transactions providing real-time access to currency trading, ensuring swift and efficient exchange.

Tom: Settlements for the next business day, perfect for planning short-term currency needs.

Today: Same-day settlement to meet urgent financial obligations and capitalize on immediate market opportunities.

Swap: Manage short-term currency exposures with agreements for simultaneous currency borrowing and lending.

Forward: Lock in exchange rates for future dates, protecting against market volatility.

DNDF: Domestic Non-Deliverable Forward contracts settled in local currency.

Bonds and Securities

Surat Berharga Negara (SBN): Enables investment in government bonds, ensuring stable returns and low risk backed by government credit.

Surat Berharga Syariah Negara (SBSN): Facilitates investments in Islamic government bonds, adhering to Shariah principles and offering ethical investment opportunities.

Surat Berharga Syariah Negara (SBSN): Features bonds with fixed interest rates, allowing investors to plan their long-term investment strategies with predictable returns.

Additional Services: Offers tailored solutions to enhance investment strategies across varying risk profiles and time frames.

Other Services

Market Info: Supplies continuous market updates, including real-time pricing and in-depth market reviews, helping clients stay informed and proactive.

Infrastructure Handling: Expert management of financial platforms such as Reuters, Bloomberg, Voice Box, and BI-ETP, ensuring seamless data flow and market access.

Additional Services: Includes various enhancements designed to improve efficiency and decision-making processes, with solutions customized to each client’s unique requirements.
